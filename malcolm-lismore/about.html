Behind the Lens

Malcolm Lismore

Malcolm Lismore is a freelance photographer based on Scotland’s North West coast, inspired by the rugged landscapes and diverse wildlife of his surroundings. With a passion for capturing the natural world, Malcolm’s portfolio spans scenic views, coastal wildlife, and Scottish heritage. In addition to his nature photography, Malcolm brings his unique style to weddings, portraits, and special events, creating lasting memories through his lens.
